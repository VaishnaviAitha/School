J.M.J High School

Welcome to our campus...

Brief History of the School: JMJ High School, Vidyanagar, Reddygudem, Alair, classes I – V along with Montessori House of Children was started in the year 2001, by the Society of Jesus, Mary, Joseph Hyderabad Province, at the invitation given by Bishop of Nalgonda. At our arrival the people of Aler were very happy as they awaited and hoped to have a good English medium school in Aler. The management has upgraded the school up to class X with two sections in each class. JMJ High School aims to provide the Education, based on value system, the best way possible to the students irrespective of caste, creed and religion and to foster the spirit of unity, enlightenment, to become responsible and citizen of our country. At present the strength of the school is 850. There are good admissions that we get from various villages. We are happy about the co - operation that the parents and the local people give us. Though the school is equipped with good infrastructure and various other facilities, one of the threatening problems that we could not solve was the presence of chloride in the water but we are trying to contact different persons in order to get some help for bore well as well as water purifier.

Facilities

Library

Libraries are another place for children to develop. They can assist students with the web, offer an area that is quiet for students to focus on their studies, and also encourage learners to take time to study.

Smart Class

Smart class technology helps students make presentations online and seek input from their teachers in less time than the traditional learning process. The same applies to teachers who do not have to advise students to make rough notes since it is easy to share the presentation directly.

Computer

Computer in education have been known to improve the performance of the students by helping them to engage better with their school work. It's believed that computer in education completely enhance students' mathematical knowledge, critical thinking.

Sports

Sports will keep their mind fresh, physically fit, and add discipline to their study routine. Skills such as coordination, multitasking and situational awareness will help in enhancing their academic skills.

Principle's Desk

JMJ High School, Aler has achieved a great land mark in the field of Education. In every sphere it has progressed and provided the children the energy and enthusiasm to develop all their talents with a holistic approach. The slogan “Love, Joy and Service “has been inspiring our students always to develop socially, economically, culturally

Vission & Mission

Vision: JMJ educational institutions are committed to empower and liberate the young (youth) through value based quality education and aspire to be a catalyst for holistic and integrated development, guided to participate in the transition of the global community and acquiring noble principles of life and service. Mission statement of the School: To mould our students into powerful human resources who can serve the society in varied environment through all stages of their life aided by unconditional love, self respect, innovative and student centered learning on par with global standards

Objectives

The Soc. of JMJ from the very beginning has given top priority to the education of youth and is conscious that this is the vital aim and charism of its Founder Rev. Fr. Mathias Wolff S.J. The Founder founded the Soc. of JMJ with the sole aim of imparting education for girls and young women, this being the need of the hour in 18/19 centuries in Holland. Inspired by the Founder’s charism, the JMJ sisters dedicate themselves and make persistent and persevering endeavors: To impart an education for an integral formation through different disciplines of knowledge. To prepare the youth for a future career
