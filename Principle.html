Principal's Desk

Principle's Desk:

Sr. Jaya Bhargavi

Dear Parents,

JMJ High School, Aler has achieved a great land mark in the field of Education. In every sphere it has progressed and provided the children the energy and enthusiasm to develop all their talents with a holistic approach. The slogan “Love, Joy and Service “has been inspiring our students always to develop socially, economically, culturally and educationally. This is possible by the blessings of the Almighty God and the selfless service of our dedicated staff. The School management believes in self discipline and encourages the students and staff to maintain health and cheerful atmosphere. All the necessary facilities in respect of regular study course, sports, culture, and extracurricular activities etc.are provided for the all-round development of the students, Supplementary course in computer science is also introduced to enable the students to shape their career prospects.


Set a goal and have a vision. Focus totally on the goal and vision and command yourself to do exactly what you want to do. If you focus the full power of all you are on what you have, a burning desire to achieve, you will attain success. Yesterday is gone but today is in our hand. When we give our all TODAY and look forward to give tomorrow the same, then there is no reason for regretting our yesterdays. We are sure to actualize the vision.

So, let us, home and school, teacher and parent join hands and together provide some meaningful education in life skills to our children. Do remain in touch with us through the almanac which provides that very vital link between the home and the school. Use it effectively and help us to give our best to your children. We await your valuable contribution in all our programmes and thank you for being an equal partner in the education of your child.
