Visiting Hours

Visiting Hours For Parents

The School would like the Parents to maintain a close rapport with the teachers teaching their wards in the school. Parents are advised to meet the Teachers and Principal on the below mentioned days.


Visiting Hours

Principal: 9.30 am to 11 am & 3 pm to 4 pm on all working days.

Teachers: 3:30 pm to 4:15 pm . On every 1st & 3rd Saturday of Month School Office Hours


School Office Hours

The School Office will remain open on all working days from 9.00 a.m. to 1.00 p.m. and 2:00 pm to 4:00 pm No business will be transacted on holidays.
