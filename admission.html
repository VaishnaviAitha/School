Admission Policy

Admission Policy


Admission to the school is reserved entirely to the school authorities. The application for the admission form shall be accompanied by an authentic document containing the child's date of birth. we accept the date of birth certificate only from the corporation, affidavit or hospital certificated will not be accepted. A student coming from an affiliated and recognized school should produce a T.C. with a clear mention date of birth. Date of birth and name once registered will not be altered. The Principal reserves to herself the right to refuse admission to any student without furnishing any reason.

Withdrawal Policy


If a student is to be withdrawn due to change of residence of domicile or for any other reasons, the T.C. will be issued or one week's notice given in writing by the parents. Pupils discontinuing after July shall pay for the entire year.

The school reserves to itself the right in dismiss the student whose diligence or progress in studies is steadily unsatisfactory or whose conduct is harmful to other students or whose guardians still show little interest in the progress of their wards. Immorality/grave in subordination/ contempt of authority/disrespect towards the members of the staff/willful damage to property is always a sufficient reason for immediate dismissal.
